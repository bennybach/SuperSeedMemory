SuperSeed Memory

Time: 100s
Score: 0

Rules

Get as many points in 100 seconds by finding the Seeds and the Super Seeds on the board.

Each pair of Seeds grants you 3 seconds extra time and 3 points.

Each pair of Super Seeds grants you 15 seconds extra time and 10 points.

All other cryptocurrencies are worth 1 point and grant no extra time.

If you clear the board with time to spare each remaining second is worth 5 points
